Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yest

  • 13/4 cups lukewarm water
  • 1/4 extra virgin olive oil. plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brudhing on top

  • 11/2 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
